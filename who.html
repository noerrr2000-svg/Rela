Who we are?

About Rela

The Rela Initiative aims to provide a comfortable and inspiring work environment for employees. We believe that providing a balanced work environment that promotes physical and mental health is the foundation for achieving productivity and innovation in any organization. We strive to provide everything necessary to ensure employee well-being through comprehensive support for mental and physical health, in addition to promoting a culture of work-life balance. This initiative aims to provide a work environment where employees feel valued and supported, contributing to increased satisfaction and sustained performance. Our philosophy at Rela revolves around emphasizing the importance of caring for employees as individuals, as their happiness and life balance positively impact work and collective performance. We believe that employee care is not a luxury, but rather a vital investment that enhances performance efficiency and increases productivity within an organization. Through Rela, we aim to build a work environment focused on employee well-being and provide full support to achieve sustainable success for all stakeholders in the workplace.

Hail Region Municipality

This initiative aims to promote the overall health of Hail Region Municipality employees and ensure they receive the support and resources they need to succeed in and outside of the workplace. This leads to improved productivity and job satisfaction, and a healthy work environment for all.

Our Vision

To create a work environment where employee well-being is prioritized, ensuring a balanced and productive workplace. We strive to be a leading example in promoting health, happiness, and work-life harmony within every organization. Rela

Our Mission

We aim to provide a healthy and balanced work environment that ensures the well-being of employees, enhancing their productivity and comfort. We believe that supporting mental and physical health boosts performance and fosters an inspiring and effective workplace.
